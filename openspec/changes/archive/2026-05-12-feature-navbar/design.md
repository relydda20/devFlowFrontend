# Design System: Navbar

## Colors & Effects
- **Primary Background**: `#0a0c14` (Deep Navy/Black).
- **Glassmorphism**: `rgba(10, 12, 20, 0.8)` with `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`.
- **Accent Color**: `#B3C5FF` (Soft Blue).
- **Transitions**: All background and text color changes MUST have a `duration-300` ease-in-out transition.

## Typography
- **Links**: Small, medium weight (`text-sm font-medium`).
- **Brand**: Bold, sans-serif, white.
