## Why

The landing page cards already communicate the right information, but their presentation could feel more intentional and polished. We want the card section to read like a designed product surface rather than a generic feature grid.

## What Changes

- Refine the landing page card section styling.
- Improve the card surface depth and separation from the background.
- Tighten spacing and alignment inside each card.
- Keep the existing card content, metrics, and layout structure intact.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `landing-cards`: A polished feature-card presentation for the landing page with stronger surface depth, spacing, and visual balance.

### Modified Capabilities
- None.

## Impact

- Affects the landing page card section in `src/pages/Landing.tsx`.
- May involve small adjustments to the shared card primitive styling.
- No routing, content, or behavior changes are required.
