## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Polished feature cards
The landing page MUST render the existing three feature cards with a more polished surface treatment that improves depth and separation from the background.

#### Scenario: Visitor views the feature grid
- **WHEN** the landing page renders
- **THEN** the three cards appear with stronger visual separation and a more intentional surface appearance

### Requirement: Balanced card spacing
The landing page MUST keep the feature card content and structure intact while tightening spacing so the icon, title, description, and footer read as a balanced unit.

#### Scenario: Visitor inspects a feature card
- **WHEN** a card is visible
- **THEN** the icon, title, body copy, and footer feel evenly spaced and aligned

### Requirement: Preserve content and layout
The system MUST preserve the current three-card grid, card copy, and overall landing page section order while applying the visual polish.

#### Scenario: Visitor compares before and after
- **WHEN** the updated landing page is rendered
- **THEN** the card count, card text, and section placement remain unchanged
