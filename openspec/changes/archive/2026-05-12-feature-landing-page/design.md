# Design System: Landing Page & Footer

## Colors & Theme
- **Main Background**: `#0E1322` (Deep Navy).
- **Card Background**: Slightly lighter than main, e.g., `#161B2B` or `bg-slate-900/50` with a subtle border (`border-white/5`).
- **Primary Accent / CTA**: `#B3C5FF` (Soft Blue).
- **Card Icon Accents**: 
  - Code Churn: Deep Blue (`#1E3A8A` or similar).
  - Context Switching: Purple (`#4C1D95` or similar).
  - AI Efficiency: Emerald Green (`#059669` or similar).
- **Status Text Colors**: Green (`text-emerald-400`), Orange/Red (`text-orange-400`).

## Typography
- **Headings**: `#F8FAFC` (White/Slate 50), bold, tracking-tight.
- **Body/Descriptions**: `#94A3B8` (Slate 400), readable contrast against `#0E1322`.
- **Micro-copy**: Monospace font (`font-mono`) for the sub-text below the CTA and the card footer metrics.
